IDMA3 is an established GRC, information and business security consultancy focused on leveraging industry and functional expertise to provide sound pragmatic advice as well as leadership enabling practical solutions for the security and GRC challenges facing both private and public sector organizations today.

At IDMA3 our solutions are based on a holistic risk-based approach to GRC and cyber security needs as a component of your organizational goals. From compliance programs and audit services to security architecture roadmaps and enterprise risk reduction plans, our solutions and services are designed to transform security from a business requirement into a business enabler.

Throughout our service offerings the primary goal is to provide clients sound guidance and solutions designed to address their specific requirements, never losing sight of the fact that security and technology must meet the needs of the organization. Not the other way around.

By identifying where an organization creates value and analyzing potential threats, IDMA3 can design focused comprehensive GRC and cyber improvement programs to meet the needs of your organization today and into the future.
