A Legal Disclaimer

The explanations and information provided on this page are general and high-level explanations and information on how to write your own Privacy Policy. You should not rely on this as legal advice. We recommend seeking legal counsel and tailoring your Privacy Policy to your data practices and jurisdictions.

Privacy Policy — The Basics

A privacy policy is a statement that discloses how a website collects, uses, discloses, processes, and manages the data of its visitors and customers...

What to Include

  • Types of information collected, and how
  • Reasons for collection
  • Sharing practices with third parties
  • Visitor rights and how to exercise them
  • Practices regarding minors' data
